## Expressions

1. work on ~ 하는중  
   ex. I am working on a new project.
2. screw + O + up 망치다  
   ex. my this year's plan is too hard to complete. I am about to screwed it up  
3. hangover (n) : 숙취로 죽어 있는 것. (술병)  
   ex. I drank too much yester day, so I could not avoid a hangover 
4. sober : 술에서 깨다 (약한 숙취)  
   ex. You can slowly sober up if you drink less than 1 unit per hour
5. once every (기간) (기간마다)  
   ex. I go to cafe for take a tea once every one weeks
6. force to ones ~ : 누군가의 ~ 를 강요하다  
   ex. You've just got to force yourself to sit down and concentrate
7. eventhough : ~ 일지라도/ ~ 임에도 불구하고   
   ex. eventhough it might be common sense in your workspace, it doesn't mean that it also common sense in other place. 
8. I think so  
9. state one's opinion  
   ex. He state his opinion strongly
10. not really 딱히 ?
11. I don't talk to a person who has a different thought to me
12. a person : (it could be prononce as aei- person)
13. I am comming to understand ~ 's mind
14. A anything else [besides, except, but] B : B 외에는 A 하지 않는다  
    ex. school doesn't teach anything else except related to study
15. typical / traditional
16. feel + O + like : O 를 ~ 처럼 느끼다  
    ex. I want to have an opportunity to drive a racecar on a track before 40,  
    it is cool and I feels it like awesome to me.
